## ğŸ“ Scenario 1: SEO

**Question:**
A company wants more website visitors without running paid ads. Which digital marketing strategy should they use? Explain.

**Detailed Answer:**
The company should use **Search Engine Optimization (SEO)**. SEO helps a website rank higher on search engines like Google through **organic (unpaid) results**. By optimizing keywords, website content, meta tags, and improving site speed and mobile friendliness, the website becomes more visible to users searching for related products or services. SEO is cost-effective and provides long-term traffic and credibility. Therefore, SEO is the best strategy when the goal is to increase visitors without paid advertising.

---

## ğŸ“ Scenario 2: Social Media Marketing

**Question:**
A clothing brand wants to engage young users and showcase products visually. Which digital marketing strategy should be used and why?

**Detailed Answer:**
The clothing brand should use **Social Media Marketing**, especially platforms like **Facebook and Instagram**. Young users spend a significant amount of time on social media, and these platforms support visual content such as images, reels, and videos. Social media marketing allows brands to engage users through likes, comments, shares, and direct messages. It also helps in building brand awareness, increasing engagement, and driving traffic to the website. Hence, social media marketing is ideal for visually appealing products like clothing.

---

## ğŸ“ Scenario 3: Email Marketing

**Question:**
An eCommerce store wants to remind users about products left in their shopping cart. Which strategy should be used?

**Detailed Answer:**
The best strategy is **Email Marketing**, specifically **abandoned cart emails**. Email marketing allows businesses to send personalized messages directly to customers. By reminding users about products left in their cart, the company can encourage them to complete the purchase. Email marketing has a high return on investment (ROI), supports personalization, and helps increase conversions and customer retention. Therefore, it is the most effective strategy for this scenario.

## ğŸ“ Scenario 4: LinkedIn Marketing

LinkedIn marketing helps generate quality leads and build professional credibility, making it the best choice for selling services to other companies.

## ğŸ“ Scenario 5: Content Marketing

**Detailed Answer:**
The brand should use **Content Marketing**. Content marketing focuses on creating valuable and informative content such as blogs, videos, guides, and case studies. ## ğŸ“ Scenario 6: Value Journey

**Question:**

**Detailed Answer:**
The company should focus on the **Customer Value Journey**. The value journey explains how a customer moves from awareness to becoming a loyal promoter of the brand. By following this journey, businesses can build long-term relationships, increase customer lifetime value, and create loyal customers.
